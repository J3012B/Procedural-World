Position: X: 0 Y: 0

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your knight

Controller support: Use D-pad or left stick to move

Press SPACE or X button to attack with your sword

Press M key or Y button to toggle minimap

Press P key to toggle 8-bit epic music

Your knight will use a boat to cross water (moves slower)

You have 3 hearts - after taking damage you're invulnerable for 2 seconds

Combat Mode activates when enemies are nearby - watch for indicators!

Enemies take multiple hits to defeat - watch for critical hits!

Beware of goblins - they're tough and can resist damage!

Defeat enemies to earn points! Chain kills for combo multipliers!
